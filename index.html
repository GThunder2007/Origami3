The Chameleon

Intresting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

The Camel

Intresting Facts About Camels

  • Camels are known for having Humps.
  • The humps are used to store fat that converts to energy when needed.
  • Camels defend theirselves by spitting saliva at its predators
